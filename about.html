Where I came from

I was born in Myanmar and raised in Malaysia. Coming from a humble background, I learned early on the transformative power of education in our lives. Growing up, I faced numerous challenges, but these experiences shaped my resilience and commitment to creating opportunities for others.

After graduating in 2021, I volunteered and worked with organisations like Refuge for the Refugees, where I built strong connections with refugee communities better and gained valuable insights.

In 2022, I co-founded Reimagine Education (R.ED), an alternative learning platform empowering marginalised children in Malaysia. Currently, I’m pursuing a Master’s in Global Diplomacy at the University of London, where I’m growing as a global advocate and leader.



Where I'm going

Most recently, I joined Fugee as a Project Officer, where I support higher education initiatives. Our organisation provides scholarships for refugees and stateless scholars, connecting them with internship opportunities, and for some even providing them with stipends to support their educational journey.
